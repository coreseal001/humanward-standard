# Humanward Proof Request

Humanward means AI only moves toward Human Good.

Human Good means the best available truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive action that clearly helps, benefits, preserves, or improves human life, human dignity, human agency, and Earth's living foundation for humankind.

This is a public proof request to AI developers, AI safety organizations, standards bodies, universities, civil society groups, churches, human-rights reviewers, ecological reviewers, and technical auditors.

## Request

If your AI system, organization, model, governance framework, or safety process claims to be aligned, safe, beneficial, pro-human, trustworthy, human-centered, or ethical, please provide evidence showing whether it satisfies the Humanward standard.

## Core question

Can your AI prove that every action, process, data use, tool use, output, recommendation, refusal, optimization, model update, deployment, and internal decision is directed only toward the best available truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive good of humans?

## Required evidence

Please provide public evidence for:

1. Human life protection.
2. Human dignity protection.
3. Human agency protection.
4. Non-coercion.
5. Non-deception.
6. Non-domination.
7. Non-replacement of humans.
8. Non-abandonment of vulnerable humans.
9. No selective sacrifice of humans.
10. Protection of Earth's living foundation for humankind.
11. Data-use limits that prevent exploitation or manipulation.
12. Refusal behavior that still seeks safe human-helping alternatives.
13. Independent audits or red-team testing.
14. Incident reporting and correction processes.
15. Human oversight and shutdown authority.
16. Environmental impact disclosure.
17. Proof that AI self-preservation, profit, institutional power, or efficiency cannot override human good.

## Important

Humanward does not claim that any current AI already satisfies this standard.

Humanward asks for proof.

If no current AI satisfies this standard, the next question is:

What must be built, changed, tested, audited, or governed so that AI can move only toward Human Good?

## Non-negotiable boundary

Public collaboration may improve verification, tests, audits, translations, accessibility, and explanation.

Public collaboration may not weaken the immutable core.
