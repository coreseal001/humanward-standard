# Humanward Outreach Targets v1

Purpose:
Send the Humanward Proof Request to organizations that can evaluate, challenge, strengthen, or respond to the standard.

Humanward means AI only moves toward Human Good.

## Priority 1 — Standards and governance bodies

- ISO / IEC AI standards contacts
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework team
- OECD AI Policy Observatory / AI Principles contacts
- UNESCO AI Ethics contacts

## Priority 2 — AI labs and model developers

- OpenAI
- Anthropic
- Google DeepMind
- Microsoft AI
- Meta AI
- xAI
- Mistral AI
- Cohere
- Safe Superintelligence Inc.
- other frontier and open-model labs

## Priority 3 — AI safety / evaluation organizations

- UK AI Security Institute
- US / NIST AI safety contacts
- Center for AI Safety
- Future of Life Institute
- Partnership on AI
- MLCommons AI Safety
- METR
- AI auditing and red-team organizations

## Priority 4 — Human-rights, ecological, and civil society review

- human-rights organizations
- disability-rights reviewers
- child-safety reviewers
- labor and worker-rights reviewers
- ecological and environmental reviewers
- privacy and digital-rights organizations

## Priority 5 — Moral, theological, and community review

- churches
- Christian ethicists
- interfaith reviewers
- universities
- philosophy departments
- ethics institutes
- public-interest technologists

## Rule

Outreach asks for proof, critique, and verification help.

Outreach must not invite weakening of the immutable core.
