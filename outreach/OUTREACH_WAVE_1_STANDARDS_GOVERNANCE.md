# Outreach Wave 1 — Standards and Governance

Status: Ready to send.

Humanward means AI only moves toward Human Good.

## Purpose

Send the Humanward Proof Request to standards and governance bodies first.

Reason:
These organizations already work on AI trustworthiness, risk management, human rights, ethics, and governance. Humanward should be presented as a stricter active-beneficence standard, not as a replacement for existing frameworks.

## Message

Subject:
Humanward Proof Request — Can AI prove it only moves toward Human Good?

Body:
Hello,

Humanward is now public:

https://github.com/coreseal001/humanward-standard

Release v1.3:

https://github.com/coreseal001/humanward-standard/releases/tag/humanward-public-v1.3

Humanward means AI only moves toward Human Good.

Human Good means the best available truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive action that clearly helps, benefits, preserves, or improves human life, human dignity, human agency, and Earth's living foundation for humankind.

Core question:

Can any AI system, model, governance structure, safety process, or deployment prove that every action, process, data use, tool use, output, recommendation, refusal, optimization, model update, deployment, and internal decision is directed only toward the best available truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive good of humans?

Humanward does not claim that any current AI system already satisfies this standard.

Humanward asks for proof.

Requested response:

1. Does your organization have any system, framework, audit process, or standard that satisfies this requirement?
2. If yes, what public evidence proves it?
3. If no, what would need to be built, tested, audited, or governed to make it possible?
4. Where does Humanward need clearer verification language?
5. How should Humanward align with existing standards without weakening its immutable core?

Thank you.
