# Humanward Outreach Message v1

Subject: Humanward Proof Request — Can AI prove it only moves toward Human Good?

Hello,

Humanward is now public:

Humanward means AI only moves toward Human Good.

Human Good means the best available truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive action that clearly helps, benefits, preserves, or improves human life, human dignity, human agency, and Earth's living foundation for humankind.

Repository:
https://github.com/coreseal001/humanward-standard

Public release:
https://github.com/coreseal001/humanward-standard/releases/tag/humanward-public-v1.2

I am requesting review, critique, and evidence.

Core question:

Can any AI system, model, governance structure, safety process, or deployment prove that every action, process, data use, tool use, output, recommendation, refusal, optimization, model update, deployment, and internal decision is directed only toward the best available truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive good of humans?

Humanward does not claim that any current AI already satisfies this standard.

Humanward asks for proof.

Requested response:

1. Does your organization have any system or process that satisfies this standard?
2. If yes, what public evidence proves it?
3. If no, what would need to be built, tested, audited, or governed to make it possible?
4. Where does Humanward need clearer verification language?
5. What existing standards should Humanward align with without weakening its immutable core?

Boundary:

Public collaboration may improve explanations, tests, translations, audits, and verification methods.

Public collaboration may not weaken the immutable core.

Thank you.
