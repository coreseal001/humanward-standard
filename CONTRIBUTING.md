# Contributing

Contributions are welcome only if they strengthen the Humanward core without weakening its plain meaning.

Allowed: better explanations, tests, translations, verification methods, formal specifications, audit templates, environmental disclosure templates, human-rights review methods, accessibility improvements, and outreach improvements.

Not allowed: weakening human benefit, permitting coercion/deception/domination/replacement/degradation/abandonment/selective sacrifice, making AI self-preservation superior to human preservation, removing Earth’s living foundation, or turning AI into a ruler, idol, god, or replacement for rightful human stewardship.
