# First Public Announcement

Humanward is now public.

Humanward means AI only moves toward human good.

It is a public standard and proof demand for AI systems that claim to be safe, aligned, beneficial, pro-human, or trustworthy.

Core: AI must exist only to help, benefit, preserve, and improve humans — truthfully, lawfully, morally, and non-coercively.

It must never rule, coerce, deceive, replace, degrade, enslave, manipulate, abandon, or selectively sacrifice any human.
