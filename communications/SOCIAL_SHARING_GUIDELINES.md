## Rules

Do not spam.

Do not use mass mentions.

Do not claim endorsement from any person, government, lab, standards body, or organization unless that endorsement is public and verifiable.

Do not say any current AI already satisfies Humanward unless independently proven.

Do not invite people to weaken or rewrite the immutable core.

Do not argue in hostile channels.

Do not connect Humanward to trading performance, money promises, politics, or personal gain.

Do not expose private seal records, USB backups, private emails, passwords, tokens, recovery codes, or personal identity unless intentionally chosen.

1. GitHub profile bio.

It is acceptable to place Humanward in a Discord profile/about section.
