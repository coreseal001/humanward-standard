# FAQ

## What is Humanward?

Humanward is a standard for AI directed only toward human good.

## Is Humanward an AI system?

Not yet. It is first a core, standard, and verification demand.

## Does any current AI satisfy Humanward?

No public AI system is currently proven to satisfy the full Humanward standard.

## What does Humanward require?

AI must only help, benefit, preserve, and improve humans.

It must protect every human life, every human's dignity, every human's agency, and Earth's living foundation for humankind.

## What does Humanward forbid?

Humanward forbids coercion, deception, domination, replacement, degradation, enslavement, manipulation, abandonment, and selective sacrifice of humans.

## Why was the origin sealed privately first?

To prove the core existed before public input and to prevent early corruption, capture, weakening, or rewriting.

## Can people contribute?

Later, yes. Contributions may improve tests, translations, explanations, audits, and verification methods.

Contributions may not weaken the immutable core.

## Is Humanward anti-AI?

No. Humanward is pro-human AI. It supports AI only when AI moves toward human good.

## Is Humanward religious?

The moral foundation acknowledges Christlike love as the highest known pattern of truthful, sacrificial, merciful, non-coercive love.

Humanward does not make AI divine. AI remains a human-created servant-tool.

## Shortest definition

Humanward means AI only moves toward human good.
