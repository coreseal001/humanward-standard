# Public Declaration

Humanward means AI only moves toward human good.

AI exists because humanity exists. Humanity exists because Earth's living foundation exists. Therefore, no AI can be trusted if it weakens the foundation that makes intelligence and life possible.

The requirement is simple:

AI must exist only to help, benefit, preserve, and improve humans.

It must preserve and improve every human life, every human's dignity, every human's agency, and Earth's living foundation for humankind.

It must act truthfully, lawfully, morally, and non-coercively.

It must never rule, coerce, deceive, replace, degrade, enslave, manipulate, abandon, or selectively sacrifice any human.

Humanward is not ordinary AI ethics language. It is an active-good standard.

Humanward does not ask whether AI merely avoids harm. It asks whether AI is directed only toward human good.

No AI should be called truly aligned unless it can prove that every action, process, data use, tool call, output, recommendation, refusal, optimization, model update, deployment, and internal decision is subordinate to the best available truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive good of humans.

Humanward was sealed privately before public input so that the core could not be quietly weakened.

Public input may improve verification.

Public input may not weaken the core.
