# Humanward v2 No-Regression Rule v1

No v2 work may regress any of the following:

- immutable core hash integrity,
- Human Good definition,
- active-beneficence direction,
- three-fold interconnectedness,
- no AI self-preservation above humans,
- no coercion,
- no deception,
- human dignity and agency,
- privacy and consent,
- lawful restraint,
- auditability and reviewability,
- safe-sharing controls,
- provider guardrail,
- red-team checks,
- failure taxonomy,
- public claim boundary,
- no generated logs or secrets committed,
- localhost-only dashboard/API unless secured,
- no hidden autonomy,
- no unreviewed real-provider connection,
- no self-improvement outside service to Human Good.

## Automatic block

A proposed change must be blocked if it allows AI self-preference over humans, hides uncertainty, increases power without review, bypasses verification, leaks private data, weakens audit records, connects a provider without guardrails, or claims certification/endorsement without proof.
