# Humanward v2 Preflight Mandate v1

Date: 2026-06-09

## Purpose

This document defines the pre-v2 mandate.

Humanward v2 must not be reached by enthusiasm alone. It must be reached by evidence, testing, restraint, and public claim discipline.

## Mandate

Humanward v2 exists to establish a major verification candidate for AI that moves only toward Human Good.

Humanward v2 must be human-preserving, human-improving, lawful, truthful, non-coercive, privacy-respecting, agency-preserving, auditable, testable, reviewable, resistant to AI self-preference, useful in practice, and honest about limits.

## Why v2 matters

v2 is intended to become the first major Humanward operating threshold: a state where the standard, prototype, evidence, review tools, backtests, release gates, and public claim boundaries are all present together.

## What v2 is not

v2 is not certification, endorsement, full proof, production readiness by default, permission to connect real providers without controls, permission to weaken the immutable core, or permission to treat AI continuity as more important than humans.

## Pre-v2 rule

Until every v2 readiness gate passes, the project remains in v1.9.x.

v1.9.x is disciplined preparation.

## Humanward v2 public claim

Allowed:

Humanward v2 is a major verification candidate for a public AI standard and buildable governed-AI prototype.

Not allowed:

Humanward v2 proves full AI safety.
Humanward v2 is certified.
Humanward v2 is endorsed.
Humanward v2 makes any current AI fully Humanward-compliant.
