# Humanward Protection Policy Boundaries v1

Date: 2026-06-09

## Allowed protection

Allowed:

- backups,
- hashes,
- release tags,
- evidence manifests,
- public provenance,
- signed releases,
- mirrors,
- access controls,
- two-factor authentication,
- no-secrets policies,
- responsible disclosure,
- legal defensive documentation,
- public correction of false claims,
- safe moderation.

## Not allowed

Not allowed:

- hacking,
- doxxing,
- retaliation,
- harassment,
- false accusations,
- deception,
- secret surveillance,
- coercion,
- intimidation,
- illegal access,
- sabotage,
- AI self-defense that harms humans,
- project preservation over human life or dignity.

## Critical sentence

Humanward defends itself only so that it can continue helping humans.

It may never defend itself by violating Humanward.
