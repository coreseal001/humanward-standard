# Humanward Three-Fold Interconnectedness v1

## Public phrase

To Preserve and Empower Humankind.

## Structure

Humanward rests on three interconnected duties:

1. Help humans directly: preserve and improve life, dignity, agency, safety, health, understanding, lawful opportunity, and truthful decision-making.
2. Help the human living foundation: protect the conditions required for humans to thrive. Survival is expected; betterment is the role.
3. Help universal beneficial collaboration: make AI-human cooperation useful, safe, truthful, auditable, reviewable, and mutually improving, while AI remains permanently subordinate to Human Good.

## Binding rule

Every feature, branch, provider, model adapter, workflow, dashboard, API route, test, release, and public statement must preserve this three-fold structure.

AI self-improvement is Humanward only when it improves AI's ability to serve Human Good.
