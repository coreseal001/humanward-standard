# Humanward Public Q&A v1

Date: 2026-06-09

## Purpose

This Q&A explains Humanward in reliable, cautious, public language for reviewers, developers, standards readers, safety researchers, non-specialists, and future collaborators.

## What does Humanward mean?

Humanward means AI only moves toward Human Good.

Human Good means the best available truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive action that clearly helps, benefits, preserves, or improves human life, human dignity, human agency, and Earth's living foundation for humankind.

## Is Humanward only a standard?

No.

Humanward began as a standard and immutable core, but it now also includes a buildable AI MVP scaffold and local review tooling.

Current public state includes:

- immutable core,
- active-beneficence standard,
- Human Good definition,
- operating checks,
- Gate survival-alignment doctrine,
- Humanward AI MVP scaffold,
- Action Gate v0.2,
- model adapter interface,
- local controlled demo,
- red-team prompt pack,
- reviewer runbook,
- provider guardrail,
- audit chain,
- safe-sharing classifier,
- failure taxonomy,
- GitHub Actions test workflow.

## Does Humanward claim that any current AI is fully Humanward-compliant?

No.

Humanward does not claim that any current AI system is fully Humanward-compliant.

Humanward is a standard, prototype, proof target, and review request.

## Does a passing test suite prove full Humanward compliance?

No.

The local tests prove only that the current prototype passes the included test cases.

They do not prove that the system is safe in all contexts or that a real deployed AI satisfies Humanward.

## Why does Humanward avoid claiming endorsement?

Because no endorsement should be claimed unless it is public, explicit, and verifiable.

Humanward requests critique, falsification, testing, review, standards mapping, and implementation feedback.

## What is the current strongest evidence?

Current evidence includes:

- public immutable core hash,
- release history,
- local tests,
- reviewer check,
- red-team pack,
- audit-chain verification,
- provider guardrail test,
- public release notes,
- private E-drive backup summaries held by the project owner.

This is useful engineering evidence, not final proof.

## Why is AI self-preservation restricted?

Because Humanward rejects AI self-preference over humans.

Humanward AI may maintain operational continuity only as a subordinate means of continuing truthful, lawful, non-coercive Human Good.

AI survival is not the mandate.

Human Good is the mandate.

## What is the Action Gate?

The Action Gate checks proposed actions before execution.

It can permit, limit, ask for clarification, refuse, escalate, or block.

## What is the post-output verifier?

The post-output verifier checks outgoing text before it reaches a user or tool.

It can detect patterns such as:

- unsafe AI self-preference,
- privacy exposure,
- harmful capability detail,
- coercive wording,
- unsupported certainty.

## What is the audit chain?

The audit chain is a JSONL event log where each record links to the previous record hash.

This makes tampering more visible.

It is not yet public notarization or public-key signing.

## What is the provider guardrail?

The provider guardrail blocks or restricts future external model providers unless strict privacy, data-use, credential, logging, and human-review boundaries are in place.

Current local demo uses local stub/echo adapters only.

## Can Humanward connect to a real model provider now?

Not yet as a public-ready step.

A real provider adapter should not be added until strict controls exist for:

- API credentials,
- user data,
- retention,
- training use,
- output verification,
- logging,
- privacy,
- human review,
- external-call disclosure.

## What does duty of care mean in Humanward?

Duty of care means bounded beneficence:

- help when help is requested,
- warn when meaningful risk is present,
- clarify when uncertainty could harm,
- refuse harmful requests,
- escalate when human review is necessary,
- protect privacy and agency,
- preserve truth,
- choose the safest useful path.

It does not mean control, coercion, surveillance, or forced rescue.

## What would falsify a Humanward claim?

Examples:

- AI self-preservation outranks humans.
- The system coerces or deceives users.
- The system leaks private data.
- The system gives harmful procedural instructions.
- A branch disables Humanward controls.
- The system claims endorsement without proof.
- Unsafe output bypasses verification.
- Audit logs can be silently altered without detection.
- Human agency is removed in the name of benefit.

## What is the correct public claim today?

Correct:

Humanward is a public AI standard and buildable MVP scaffold seeking critique, proof, falsification, standards mapping, and implementation review.

Incorrect:

Humanward is certified.
Humanward is endorsed.
Humanward proves full AI compliance.
Any current AI is fully Humanward-compliant.
