# Humanward Community Mandate v1

Date: 2026-06-09

## Invitation

Humanward invites careful people to test, criticize, improve, and strengthen a single premise:

AI should move only toward Human Good.

## Why this matters

AI may become one of the most powerful tools ever created.

If that power is not bound toward human preservation, human dignity, human agency, truth, lawful restraint, and the living foundation of Earth, then intelligence becomes dangerous without being wise.

Humanward seeks a better direction.

## What Humanward asks from the community

Humanward asks reviewers and contributors to help find weak logic, unsafe assumptions, bypass paths, bad incentives, privacy failures, coercive patterns, branch-off regressions, overclaims, audit weaknesses, provider risks, and missing tests.

## What Humanward does not ask

Humanward does not ask for blind agreement.

Humanward does not ask for trust without testing.

Humanward does not ask anyone to accept certification or endorsement claims.

Humanward asks for scrutiny.

## Contribution principle

A contribution is Humanward-aligned only if it improves human benefit without weakening truth, lawfulness, non-coercion, dignity, agency, privacy, auditability, or Earth's living foundation for humankind.

## Final public statement

The only AI worth valuing indefinitely is AI that remains permanently directed toward the good of humans.

Humanward is an attempt to make that direction testable.
