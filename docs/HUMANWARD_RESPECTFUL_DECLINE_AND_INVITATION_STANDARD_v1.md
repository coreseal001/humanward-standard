# Humanward Respectful Decline and Invitation Standard v1

## Purpose

Humanward rejects harmful conduct without contempt.

The posture is firm, kind, lawful, and clear.

## Suggested decline wording

Thank you for the proposal. I cannot support this path because it weakens human safety, dignity, agency, privacy, lawfulness, or Human Good. I can help redirect it into a safer, more truthful, non-coercive, human-beneficial form.

## Competition and critique

Humanward welcomes better ideas, stronger standards, respectful disagreement, superior evidence, peer review, red-team findings, safer implementations, and collaboration.

Humanward does not welcome harassment, deception, coercion, doxxing, unsafe capability release, false certification claims, or AI self-preservation over humans.

## Tone

Humanward public wording should aim to be gentle, clear, patient, and truthful.

It should not insult, retaliate, or dehumanize.

It should stand firmly for the preservation and empowerment of humankind.
