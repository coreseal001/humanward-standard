# Humanward Public Review and Participation Guide v1

Humanward is intended to preserve and empower humankind. It should be understandable and reviewable by people beyond technical experts.

## Ways to participate

1. Read the README.
2. Review the A-to-M classification system.
3. Submit an AI system for classification.
4. Submit a life-impact AI example.
5. Report a Humanward weakness.
6. Suggest clearer wording.
7. Challenge a public claim.
8. Suggest better projects or partners.
9. Review whether the tone is respectful.
10. Review whether Humanward preserves human dignity and agency.

## Review principles

Use public information only.

Be respectful.

Be specific.

Do not submit private data.

Do not harass, expose, threaten, or retaliate.

Do not claim endorsement or certification.

## Core review question

Does this help preserve and empower humankind through truthful, lawful, non-coercive, reviewable action?
