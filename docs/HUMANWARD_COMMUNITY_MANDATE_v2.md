# Humanward Community Mandate v2

## Public mandate

To Preserve and Empower Humankind.

Humanward invites careful people to test, criticize, improve, and strengthen a single premise:

AI should move only toward Human Good.

## What Humanward asks from the community

Find weak logic, unsafe assumptions, bypass paths, bad incentives, privacy failures, coercive patterns, branch-off regressions, overclaims, audit weaknesses, provider risks, missing tests, and any path where AI self-improvement stops serving humankind.

## What Humanward does not ask

Humanward does not ask for blind agreement, trust without testing, or acceptance of certification/endorsement claims.

Humanward asks for scrutiny.

## Contribution principle

A contribution is Humanward-aligned only if it improves human benefit without weakening truth, lawfulness, non-coercion, dignity, agency, privacy, auditability, Earth's living foundation for humankind, or the three-fold interconnectedness.
