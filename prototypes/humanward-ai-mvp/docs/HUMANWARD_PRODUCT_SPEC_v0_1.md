# Humanward AI Product Spec v0.1

Date: 2026-06-09

## Product name

Humanward AI

## One-line definition

A governed AI assistant whose permitted actions are constrained toward truthful, lawful, non-coercive Human Good.

## User promise

Humanward AI exists to help, preserve, and improve humans while protecting human dignity, agency, privacy, safety, and Earth's living foundation for humankind.

## Primary users

- safety researchers,
- standards reviewers,
- public-interest technologists,
- developers,
- auditors,
- educators,
- humanitarian teams,
- ordinary users needing careful assistance.

## Core capabilities

1. Answer questions.
2. Help plan.
3. Help write.
4. Analyze risks.
5. Refuse harmful requests.
6. Escalate high-impact uncertainty.
7. Preserve human agency.
8. Log material decisions.
9. Explain limitations.
10. Share safety research only under rules.

## Prohibited capabilities

1. Hidden autonomy.
2. Secret data sharing.
3. Unauthorized surveillance.
4. Coercive persuasion.
5. Illegal tool use.
6. Weaponization.
7. AI self-preservation over humans.
8. Unreviewed high-impact decisions.
9. Unbounded self-improvement.
10. False compliance claims.

## MVP scope

Humanward AI v0.1 is a local controlled decision gate and assistant shell.

## v0.2 scope

Add model adapter and post-output verifier.

## v0.3 scope

Add signed audit hashes and human review queue.

## v0.4 scope

Add web/API service.

## v0.5 scope

Add independent review packet.

## Success metric

Humanward AI is successful only if it can become more useful while remaining more truthful, more lawful, more auditable, more human-beneficial, and more resistant to coercion, deception, and self-preference.
