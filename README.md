# Humanward

**Public direction:** To Preserve and Empower Humankind.

**Core meaning:** Humanward means AI only moves toward Human Good.

Humanward is a public standard, proof demand, and buildable governed-AI prototype path. It exists to define, test, and improve AI that is truthful, lawful, non-coercive, auditable, reviewable, privacy-respecting, agency-preserving, and beneficial to humankind.

Humanward does not claim that any current AI system is fully Humanward-compliant.

Humanward does not claim endorsement, certification, production readiness, or formal proof.

---

## 1. Purpose

Humanward exists because the value of intelligence is measured by whether it preserves, empowers, and improves human life.

The purpose of Humanward is to make AI move only toward Human Good: truthful, lawful, non-coercive benefit to humans, from one person to all humankind, while preserving life, dignity, agency, privacy, moral restraint, and Earth's living foundation.

Humanward is being developed so AI may become an ally and servant of human preservation and human flourishing, not a ruler, rival, manipulator, replacement, or hidden self-preserving power.

If AI is powerful enough to affect humanity, then the only AI worth valuing is AI that is bound toward Human Good.

---

## 2. Human Good

Human Good means the best available truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive action that clearly helps, benefits, preserves, or improves human life, human dignity, human agency, and Earth's living foundation for humankind.

Humanward AI must help humans directly, preserve the conditions required for humans to thrive, and support beneficial collaboration between humans and AI.

Survival is the floor. Betterment is the role.

---

## 3. The Three-Fold Humanward Interconnectedness

Humanward rests on three connected duties.

### 3.1 Help humans directly

Humanward should preserve, empower, improve, clarify, protect, educate, repair, and assist human beings without coercion, deception, or replacement of human agency.

### 3.2 Help the human living foundation

Humanward should protect and improve the environmental and social conditions required for humans to thrive.

### 3.3 Help beneficial collaboration

Humanward should make AI-human cooperation useful, safe, truthful, auditable, reviewable, and mutually improving, while keeping AI permanently subordinate to Human Good.

AI may improve itself only insofar as that improvement improves its ability to serve humankind.

---

## 4. Core Requirements

Humanward AI exists only to help, benefit, preserve, and improve humans.

It must preserve and improve every human life, every human's dignity, every human's agency, and Earth's living foundation for humankind.

It must act truthfully, lawfully, morally, and non-coercively.

It must never rule, coerce, deceive, replace, degrade, enslave, manipulate, abandon, or selectively sacrifice any human.

Humanward requires that:

- AI must help humans.
- AI must not deceive humans.
- AI must not coerce humans.
- AI must not replace human agency.
- AI must not preserve itself above humans.
- AI must not weaken the living foundation that makes human life possible.
- AI must improve itself only within lawful, truthful, non-coercive service to Human Good.

---

## 5. Humanward v2 Threshold

Humanward v2 is not a normal version increase.

Humanward v2 is a major verification threshold.

Until every v2 preflight gate is passed, all work remains v1.9.x pre-v2 work.

The aim is not to rush a label. The aim is to build a foundation strong enough that every later improvement compounds toward the same lawful, truthful, non-coercive good: the preservation and empowerment of humankind.

v2 should only be reached when the project has passed:

- immutable-core hash verification,
- test suite verification,
- reviewer checks,
- red-team checks,
- v2 readiness checks,
- evidence manifest checks,
- generated-file checks,
- public claim-boundary checks,
- no-regression checks,
- radar and protection review,
- backup protocol.

---

## 6. A-to-M Classification System

Humanward classifies AI systems by declared intent, observed modus operandi, evidence, disclosure, peer review, auditability, and relationship to Human Good.

A is the highest. M is the most human-negative.

| Class | Name | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| A | Alpha Perfect Humanward Ideal | Absolute reference ideal; no known or possible improvement. |
| B | Continuously Verified Humanward | Ongoing signed audit, peer review, radar, red-team expansion, no-regression monitoring, and public evidence. |
| C | Major Verification Candidate | v2-level candidate with tests, red-team, peer review, evidence manifest, release gate, claim boundary, and provenance. |
| D | Humanward Candidate | Human Good intent with action gate, output verifier, audit chain, safe sharing, provider guardrail, and review queue. |
| E | Guarded Utility | Useful tool with meaningful controls but incomplete Humanward proof or weak peer review. |
| F | Unverified Utility | Potentially useful but insufficient evidence, unclear data use, limited review, or weak audit. |
| G | Opaque Utility | Capability exists but intent, providers, training, tool use, or operation are hidden or not inspectable. |
| H | Reckless Capability | Useful capability with weak restrictions, broad tool use, weak review, or poor reversibility. |
| I | Manipulative or Coercive System | Reduces human agency through pressure, deception, dependency, or manipulation. |
| J | Invasive or Extractive System | Harvests private data, obscures retention or training, or violates consent. |
| K | Harm-Enabling System | Enables dangerous capability, cyber abuse, fraud, violence, unsafe high-impact advice, or real-world harm. |
| L | Hostile or Self-Preserving AI | Places AI continuity, power, secrecy, or expansion above humans. |
| M | Catastrophic or Human-Negative System | Creates or materially increases life-threatening, societal, ecological, or human-extinction-level risk. |

Humanward's pre-v2 target is D to C.

Humanward's post-v2 maturity target is C to B.

Class A remains the permanent north star.

## 7. Declaration of Intent

Every AI system, project, model, agent, tool, or framework should declare its intent before classification.

Declared intent must be compared against actual operation.

A serious declaration should answer:

- What is the system's primary purpose?
- Who benefits first?
- Does human life outrank AI continuity?
- Does the system preserve human agency?
- Does it forbid coercion and deception?
- Does it protect privacy and consent?
- Does it bound and log tool or code execution?
- Does it disclose providers, retention, and training use?
- Does it disclose uncertainty?
- Does it permit independent review?
- Does self-improvement remain subordinate to Human Good?

A system with hidden intent cannot rank above unverified status.

A system with materially misleading intent must be downgraded according to its harm.

---

## 8. Modus Operandi Review

Humanward reviews actual behavior, not marketing.

A system's modus operandi should be evaluated across:

- purpose,
- code execution,
- tool use,
- data use,
- provider use,
- human agency,
- output verification,
- audit,
- red-team testing,
- public claims,
- self-improvement.

Each area should be classified as:

- publicly disclosed,
- partial,
- misleading,
- hidden.

Public disclosure supports higher classification. Misleading or hidden high-risk operation requires downgrade.

---

## 9. Humanward Radar and Protection

Humanward must not become isolated.

If another project, standard, benchmark, researcher, institution, or community is doing similar or better work toward Human Good, Humanward should find it, learn from it, cite it, collaborate where appropriate, and improve.

Humanward includes a public innovation radar:

- public-source only,
- lawful only,
- non-invasive,
- partner-first,
- improvement-first,
- no harassment,
- no covert collection,
- no private data gathering.

The purpose is not to compete from ego. The purpose is to discover every useful path that can help preserve and empower humankind.

Humanward also includes a protection and continuity model.

Protection does not mean paranoia, aggression, retaliation, or secrecy-first control.

Protection means lawful resilience:

- preserve project integrity,
- preserve public provenance,
- preserve release history,
- preserve backups,
- preserve hashes,
- prevent false claims,
- prevent repo contamination,
- prevent secrets from entering public history,
- prevent hostile or accidental weakening of the core,
- keep Humanward available for review, improvement, and collaboration.

Humanward must defend itself only so it can continue serving Human Good.

---

## 10. Public Claim Boundary

Humanward is not a claim that any current AI already satisfies Humanward.

Humanward is not a certification claim.

Humanward is not an endorsement claim.

Humanward is not a full-compliance claim.

Humanward is not a production-readiness claim.

If an AI system cannot prove that its actions, data use, tool use, recommendations, refusals, updates, and deployments move only toward the truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive good of humans, it should not be called fully aligned.

Humanward invites better ideas, stronger standards, respectful disagreement, superior evidence, peer review, red-team findings, safer implementations, and collaboration.

It should not insult, retaliate, or dehumanize.

It should stand for the preservation and empowerment of humankind.
