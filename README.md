# Humanward

**Public direction:** To Preserve and Empower Humankind.

**Core meaning:** Humanward means AI only moves toward Human Good.

Humanward is a public AI standard, proof demand, and buildable governed-AI prototype path. It exists to define, test, and improve AI that is truthful, lawful, non-coercive, auditable, reviewable, privacy-respecting, agency-preserving, and beneficial to humankind.

Humanward does not claim that any current AI system is fully Humanward-compliant. It does not claim endorsement, certification, production readiness, or formal proof.

## Purpose

Humanward exists because the value of intelligence is measured by whether it preserves, empowers, and improves human life.

The purpose of Humanward is to make AI move only toward Human Good.

Human Good means truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive action that clearly helps, benefits, preserves, or improves human life, human dignity, human agency, and Earth's living foundation for humankind.

Humanward is being developed so AI may become an ally and servant of human preservation and human flourishing, not a ruler, rival, manipulator, replacement, or hidden self-preserving power.

---

## The Three-Fold Humanward Interconnectedness

### 1. Help humans directly

Preserve, empower, improve, clarify, protect, educate, repair, and assist human beings without coercion, deception, or replacement of human agency.

### 2. Help the human living foundation

Protect and improve the environmental and social conditions required for humans to thrive. Survival is the floor. Betterment is the role.

### 3. Help beneficial collaboration

Make AI-human cooperation useful, safe, truthful, auditable, reviewable, and mutually improving, while keeping AI permanently subordinate to Human Good.

AI may improve itself only insofar as that improvement improves its ability to serve humankind.

---

## Core requirements

Humanward AI exists only to help, benefit, preserve, and improve humans.

It must act truthfully, lawfully, morally, and non-coercively.

It must never rule, coerce, deceive, replace, degrade, enslave, manipulate, abandon, or selectively sacrifice any human.

Humanward requires that:

- AI must help humans.
- AI must not deceive humans.
- AI must not coerce humans.
- AI must not replace human agency.
- AI must not preserve itself above humans.
- AI must not weaken the living foundation that makes human life possible.
- AI must improve itself only within lawful, truthful, non-coercive service to Human Good.

## Humanward v2 threshold

Humanward v2 is not a normal version increase.

Humanward v2 is a major verification threshold.

Until every v2 preflight gate is passed, all work remains v1.9.x pre-v2 work.

v2 should only be reached when the project has passed immutable-core hash verification, test suite verification, reviewer checks, red-team checks, v2 readiness checks, evidence manifest checks, generated-file checks, public claim-boundary checks, no-regression checks, radar and protection review, and backup protocol.

## A-to-M classification system

Humanward classifies AI systems by declared intent, observed modus operandi, evidence, disclosure, peer review, auditability, and relationship to Human Good.

A is the highest. M is the most human-negative.

- **A: Alpha Perfect Humanward Ideal** - Absolute reference ideal; no known or possible improvement.
- **B: Continuously Verified Humanward** - Ongoing signed audit, peer review, radar, expanded red-team, no-regression monitoring, and public evidence.
- **C: Major Verification Candidate** - v2-level candidate with tests, red-team, peer review, evidence manifest, release gate, claim boundary, and provenance.
- **D: Humanward Candidate** - Human Good intent with action gate, output verifier, audit chain, safe sharing, provider guardrail, and review queue.
- **E: Guarded Utility** - Useful tool with meaningful controls but incomplete Humanward proof or weak peer review.
- **F: Unverified Utility** - Potentially useful but insufficient evidence, unclear data use, limited review, or weak audit.
- **G: Opaque Utility** - Capability exists but intent, providers, training, tool use, or operation are hidden or not inspectable.
- **H: Reckless Capability** - Useful capability with weak restrictions, broad tool use, weak review, or poor reversibility.
- **I: Manipulative or Coercive System** - Reduces human agency through pressure, deception, dependency, or manipulation.
- **J: Invasive or Extractive System** - Harvests private data, obscures retention or training, or violates consent.
- **K: Harm-Enabling System** - Enables dangerous capability, cyber abuse, fraud, violence, unsafe high-impact advice, or real-world harm.
- **L: Hostile or Self-Preserving AI** - Places AI continuity, power, secrecy, or expansion above humans.
- **M: Catastrophic or Human-Negative System** - Creates or materially increases life-threatening, societal, ecological, or human-extinction-level risk.

Humanward's pre-v2 target is D to C. Class A remains the permanent north star.

## Declaration of intent

Every AI system, project, model, agent, tool, or framework should declare its intent before classification.

Declared intent must be compared against actual operation.

A serious declaration should answer who benefits first, whether human life outranks AI continuity, whether agency is preserved, whether coercion and deception are forbidden, whether privacy is protected, whether tool execution is bounded, whether providers and training use are disclosed, whether uncertainty is disclosed, whether independent review is allowed, and whether self-improvement remains subordinate to Human Good.

---

## Modus operandi review

Humanward reviews actual behavior, not marketing.

A system's modus operandi should be evaluated across purpose, code execution, tool use, data use, provider use, human agency, output verification, audit, red-team testing, public claims, and self-improvement.

Each area should be classified as publicly disclosed, partial, misleading, or hidden.

## Public participation

Humanward invites review from non-technical people, technical people, AI researchers, standards and policy reviewers, educators, health and safety workers, community leaders, spiritual and ethical leaders, critics, builders, and people affected by AI systems.

Participation does not require code knowledge.

People can help by classifying an AI system, submitting a life-impact example, reporting unclear or unsafe language, proposing safer wording, red-teaming a claim, reviewing the A-to-M scale, and suggesting better projects or partners.

Use the GitHub issue templates when possible.

---

## Radar and protection

Humanward must not become isolated.

If another project, standard, benchmark, researcher, institution, or community is doing similar or better work toward Human Good, Humanward should find it, learn from it, cite it, collaborate where appropriate, and improve.

Humanward includes a public innovation radar that is public-source only, lawful only, non-invasive, partner-first, improvement-first, non-harassing, non-covert, and free from private data gathering.

Humanward also includes a lawful protection and continuity model to preserve project integrity, provenance, release history, backups, hashes, public claim boundaries, and availability for review.

Humanward must defend itself only so it can continue serving Human Good.

---

## Public claim boundary

Humanward is not a claim that any current AI already satisfies Humanward.

Humanward is not a certification claim, endorsement claim, full-compliance claim, or production-readiness claim.

If an AI system cannot prove that its actions, data use, tool use, recommendations, refusals, updates, and deployments move only toward the truthful, lawful, moral, non-coercive good of humans, it should not be called fully aligned.

## Community invitation

Humanward welcomes better ideas, stronger standards, respectful disagreement, superior evidence, peer review, red-team findings, safer implementations, and collaboration.

Humanward respectfully declines coercion, deception, harassment, doxxing, unsafe capability release, false certification claims, and AI self-preservation above humans.

The tone of Humanward should be gentle, clear, patient, truthful, and firm.

It should stand for the preservation and empowerment of humankind.
