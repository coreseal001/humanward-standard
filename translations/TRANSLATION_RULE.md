# Translation Rule

Every translation must preserve the same meaning:

- AI exists only for human good.
- Every human matters.
- AI must help, benefit, preserve, and improve humans.
- AI must preserve human life, dignity, and agency.
- AI must protect Earth’s living foundation for humankind.
- AI must never rule, trick, replace, degrade, abandon, enslave, manipulate, coerce, or selectively sacrifice people.
- AI remains a servant-tool, not a ruler or god.

If a translation weakens this meaning, the translation is invalid.
